How to order

Choose your favorite dishes from pantry cafe.

  • Choose your dishes, the quantities, and add to your basket. Choose dish options when applicable.
  • Once you have everything you need, simply click on “Checkout”.
  • Log in to proceed. If you are a new customer, please take a couple of minutes to register first.
  • Schedule your delivery (immediate or for a specific date &time). Choose your payment method.
  • You can also add a new delivery address if you wish.
  • Put in your “Special Request” (light shopping, not to ring the doorbell, etc). Submit your order.

Allow approximately 45 - 60 minutes for delivery.

  • We deliver from some of Jumerah and Business restaurants within 40 minutes.
  • To guarantee a quicker delivery service we recommend that you place your order from a restaurant in your delivery area. However, if for reasons beyond our control there is going to be a delay, you will be notified.
  • If you wish to schedule a delivery for a specific time, you are welcome to place the order in advance. Please ask your operator for any further details.

Cancelling your order.

An order can only be cancelled within  5 minutes of placing it.

Delivery Times  (Subject to restaurant opening times)

Saturday to Thursday:           11:00 - 23:00

Fridays and public holidays:          12:00 - 23:00
